A medicinal plant is a plant that has similar properties to conventional pharmaceutical drugs. A pharmaceutical drug is a drug that is produced in a laboratory to cure or help an illness. Medicinal plants often have antimicrobial properties that prevent bacteria, molds, or other microbes from growing.
Medicinal plants like Ashwagandha, Tulsi, Shatavari, am/a, etc. have applications as home remedies to treat various ailments and strengthen the body, or commercially for the synthesis of drugs and cosmetic products.
